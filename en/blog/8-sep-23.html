The /e/cosystem LineageOS experience

Yeah, so I forgot to talk about the installation that went down 2 days ago.
It was okay. I didn't have many problems.
The tutorial I followed had a small problem regarding step 1 of "Flashing additional partitions" where the files with the latest date were apparently too new for the latest build for my phone, but I just downloaded older files and I was done.
Now, while using /e/ and being free of Google's shackles is great and all that, I don't like it. I just rely too much on Google services to do what I do so I can't leave it yet.
So I said "fuck it" and installed LineageOS instead.
And while I might think this is possibly the best experience I have had with an Android device ever, it still has a small problem: It does not come with Google services, you have to sideload a package through ADB that installs the Google app and Play Store, because apparently it's illegal to have them built-in since LineageOS is open source and Google apps aren't.
That was this Wednesday and I've had 2 days to test the new phone out and I gotta say, it's a great experience. If I ever change my opinion about it, I'll still have a higher regar for it than MIUI just because that cesspool has very intrusive ads.
Goodnight.
